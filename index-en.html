THE BAND樂隊介紹

Six Voices, One Skyline六種聲音，同一片天際線

Formed on the late-night rooftops and cassette-tape culture of 1980s Hong Kong, Horizon fuses driving guitar riffs with analog synth textures and dual-vocal harmonies. Think smoke-filled clubs, neon signage, and choruses built to be shouted back.

Today the band tours internationally, carrying that same restless, city-lights energy to every stage they play.

地平線樂隊誕生於一九八零年代香港的天台夜色與卡式帶文化之中，將強勁的結他riff、模擬合成器音色與雙主音和聲融為一體。菸霧瀰漫的Live House、閃爍的霓虹招牌，還有觀眾齊聲吶喊的副歌 — 這就是他們的舞台。

如今樂隊已踏上國際巡演之路，將那份不羈的都市霓虹能量，帶到世界各地的舞台。
